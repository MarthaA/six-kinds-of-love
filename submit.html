    Question List

    • Personal
    • Are you taking care of yourself emotionally?
    • Are you putting enough effort into your relationships?
    • What are the relationships in your life that drain your energy? 
    • What is the relationship that causes you the most shame or guilt? 
    • Are you holding on to someone that you need to let go of? 
    • Family
    • How is your relationship with your parents?
    • What is the most important relationship you have in your family?
    • How was your childhood home life?
    • How will you raise your children differently than your parents did?
    • Who do you wish you could’ve had a different relationship within your family?
    • Friendship
    • How many people do you truly feel close to?
    • What was the strangest/most interesting beginning to a friendship?
    • What do you do to “hang-out”? Netflix, bars, skype, eat, etc.?
    • What could you have done to save a friendship?
    • What’s the nicest thing a friend has ever done for you?
    • Love
    • What do you consider love?
    • What is the sweetest thing someone has done for you?
    • What’s your favorite memory with a significant other?
    • What was your first kiss like?
    • What is one thing you regret doing in a relationship?
    • Misc
    • What kind of relationship do you value the most?
    • What relationship has made you feel the most passionate and alive? 
    • What kind of relationship do you wish you could have and why?
