    about

    What do people think of their relationships? Regret? Love? Infatuation?

    Six Kinds of Love was created to find, categorize, and arrange personal beliefs on different types of relationships. We asked strangers their thoughts and feelings on different types of relationships in order to better understand how relationships are their own entity. We developed six different categories and at least three questions per category before asking strangers to handwrite their answers. These answers were then categorized and added to the website.

    Relationships have evolved through the years while the word, ‘love,’ has been reduced to a single meaning. Traditionally, the ancient Greeks believed that love was too complicated to be compiled to a single word. Six different kinds of love existed.
